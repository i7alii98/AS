Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To educate our young within a happy, caring and culturally diverse environment so that they are able to achieve their full potential while building self-esteem and developing healthy minds.

Our Mission

Ahlia School offers a balanced, broad, and meaningful academic curriculum striving for academic excellence, supported by a co-curricular program for holistic learner development.

About Ahlia School

Established in 2010 in Saar, Kingdom of Bahrain, Ahlia School has grown to serve students from Pre-school through Grade 12, following the American curriculum combined with high-quality international education standards.

Academic Programs

We offer a balanced education in English and Arabic across all disciplines, ensuring comprehensive student development.

Our Preschool is designed to meet the needs of young children in a bilingual environment. The curriculum promotes developmental activities that support emotional, social, physical, and cognitive growth.

Arab Reading Challenge Champion - 2nd Place winner

A proud moment for Ahlia School. Mohammed Jassim Mubarak from Al Ahlia School has made history, securing 2nd place in the Arab Reading Challenge — outshining over 32 million participants from 50 countries. His achievement reflects Bahrain’s continued excellence in education and the nation’s commitment to nurturing a generation that loves knowledge, language, and learning.
